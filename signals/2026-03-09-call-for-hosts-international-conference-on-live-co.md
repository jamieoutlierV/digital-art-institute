# 2026-03-09-call-for-hosts-international-conference-on-live-co

The rotating host model is a form of practitioner-led infrastructure governance that resists institutional centralisation, distributing visibility and labour across geographies. As a signal, it confirms live coding's ongoing commitment to communal stewardship and liveness as both artistic and organisational principle, while surfacing the resource constraints that shape how decentralised creative communities sustain their own institutions.
